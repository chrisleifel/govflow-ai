Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

All plans come with a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades occur at the end of your billing period.

Are there any setup fees?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans may include implementation fees depending on customization requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and purchase orders for Enterprise customers.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. For example, the Professional plan is $1,199/month when billed annually ($14,388/year).

What happens if I exceed my user or citizen account limits?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional seats at $49/user/month for staff and $0.10/month per citizen account.

Do you offer non-profit or educational discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualifying non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for details.

What's your data security and compliance certification?

We maintain SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA compliance, and CJIS certification. Enterprise plans include FedRAMP authorization.
