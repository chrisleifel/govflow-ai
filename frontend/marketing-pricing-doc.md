## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's included in the free trial?
All plans come with a 30-day free trial with full access to all features in that plan tier. No credit card required to start. You'll have access to:
- All platform features for your plan level
- Email and chat support
- Training resources and documentation
- Sample workflows and templates
- Onboarding assistance

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time:
- **Upgrades** take effect immediately with prorated billing
- **Downgrades** occur at the end of your current billing period
- No penalties for plan changes
- Keep your data and configuration

### Are there any setup fees?
- **Starter Plan:** No setup fees
- **Professional Plan:** No setup fees
- **Enterprise Plan:** May include implementation fees depending on customization requirements (typically $5,000-$25,000)

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept multiple payment options:
- All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- ACH bank transfers
- Wire transfers
- Purchase orders (Enterprise customers)
- Government procurement cards (P-Cards)
- Annual prepayment (20% discount)

### Is there a discount for annual billing?
Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually:
- **Starter:** $399/month ($4,788/year) - Save $1,200
- **Professional:** $1,199/month ($14,388/year) - Save $3,600
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing with volume discounts

### What happens if I exceed my user or citizen account limits?
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limits (at 80% and 95%). You can:
- Upgrade to the next plan tier
- Purchase additional seats:
  - Staff users: $49/user/month
  - Citizen accounts: $0.10/account/month
- We provide a 10% grace period before any overage charges apply

### Do you offer non-profit or educational discounts?
Yes, we offer special pricing:
- **Non-profit organizations:** 25% discount on all plans
- **Educational institutions:** 30% discount on all plans
- **Multi-year contracts:** Additional 10% discount
- **Multi-agency partnerships:** Volume discounts available

Must provide proof of 501(c)(3) status or educational accreditation.
